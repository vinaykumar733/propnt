Common Questions

Is PropNT a property listing site?

No. We don't list properties in the traditional sense. We operate an AI-powered discovery layer where supply and demand meet through controlled conversation, not open directories.

How does the AI understand what I need?

You speak naturally. The AI extracts structured intent—location preferences, budget ranges, timelines, flexibility—and matches against verified supply signals. It asks clarifying questions when needed, like a skilled human assistant.

How do I see actual property details?

Details unlock through qualification. First, AI conversation. Then, verification. Finally, mediated access to comprehensive information and owner contact.

Who are the mediators?

StayBroker verified agents. Humans who verify documents, structure expectations, and facilitate clean handoffs between serious parties. The AI handles discovery; humans handle trust.

What does this cost?

AI-powered discovery is open. Qualification is free. Mediation carries a success-aligned fee, disclosed only after intent is confirmed. No surprise charges.
